# Cryptography Essays Portfolio

This repository contains two independent mathematical essays written as part of my studies, focusing on modern cryptographic systems based on elliptic curves and isogenies.

---

## Overview

These essays explore key areas of post-quantum and elliptic-curve-based cryptography, with emphasis on both theoretical structure and cryptographic application.

They are intended as standalone expository pieces demonstrating mathematical understanding and communication of advanced topics.

---

## Essays Included

### 1. Elliptic Curve Cryptography (ECC)
- Topics covered: elliptic curves over finite fields, group law, discrete logarithm problem, and cryptographic applications.
- Focus: foundational structure of ECC and why it is secure for classical cryptography.
- [File](ecc.pdf)

---

### 2. Isogeny-Based Cryptography
- Topics covered: isogenies between elliptic curves, supersingular isogeny graphs, and post-quantum cryptographic schemes.
- Focus: motivation for isogeny-based systems and their role in post-quantum security.
- [File](isogeny-based-crypto.pdf)
